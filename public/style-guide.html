The Alpha-Tint Pattern

POAST rarely uses solid accent backgrounds. Pattern: accent + alpha-suffix-hex. Common suffixes:

+ 08 · 3% — barely-visible row tint
+ 12 · 7% — chip background
+ 40 · 25% — focused border
+ 60 · 38% — selected border

Notes on the transitions:

Hover
Border tints amber (~40% alpha), shadow lifts with a low-alpha amber bloom, the element rises 1–2px. Duration 200ms ease.
Focus
3px amber halo at 20% alpha + a 12–16px outer glow. Border goes solid amber. Visible without color (works in high-contrast mode).
Selected
Background gets a 5–8% amber wash. Border solid amber at ~50% alpha. Outer glow but no lift — selection isn't motion.

Voice & Copy

SECTION 09

Visual style is half the brand. Copy discipline is the other half. Side-by-side: what AI tends to write vs. how POAST should sound.

DON'T · BOOMER / AI
"In this episode, we dive deep into the revolutionary world of AI infrastructure — exploring everything you need to know about how next-gen chips are transforming the future of compute."
"dive deep", "revolutionary", "everything you need to know", "next-gen", "transforming", em dash. Six tells in one sentence.
DO · DIRECT, SPECIFIC
"Doug and Jordan walk through Blackwell yields, why HBM3E is the bottleneck, and what TSMC's CoWoS roadmap means for inference cost in 2026."
Names, numbers, specific stakes. No connective tissue, no scaffolding.
DON'T · CLICKBAIT
"Why memory is the next bottleneck (and what nobody's telling you)"
Vague. Withholds the answer. Rhetorical "why" opener.
DO · HARD CLAIM
"HBM3E is sold out through 2026. Here's what that does to GPU pricing."
Concrete claim up top. The reader knows what they're getting.
DON'T · CTA SLOP
"Don't miss out — unleash the full potential of POAST today! 🚀"
"Don't miss out", "unleash", emoji. Three tells, four if you count the rocket.
DO · NEUTRAL CTA
"Open POAST and pick a tool. Slop Top is the fastest if you have a link."
Tells the user exactly what to do. Recommends a path. No urgency theatre.
DON'T · TITLE SCAFFOLD
"A Deep Dive Into Vera Rubin: Everything You Need To Know"
"Deep dive" + "everything you need to know". The two most-flagged AI-coded title scaffolds.
DO · CLAIM AS TITLE
"Vera Rubin Doubles HBM Bandwidth and Halves Power Per Token"
A claim that could only have come from someone who knows the chip.
The hard rules
No em dashes
Use commas, periods, or colons.
No emojis
In product copy. Use lucide icons for symbolic emphasis.
No hype words
Banned: revolutionary, unleash, game-changing, next-gen, transform, dive into, unlock, seamless.
Lead with a fact
Plain declarative sentences. No "Why X matters" rhetorical openers.
Sentence case
Body uses sentence case. Title Case only for proper nouns and tile labels.
Caps labels are mono
JetBrains Mono, 700 weight, 1.5–2px letter-spacing, uppercase. Replaces h3/h4.

AI Pairing — paste this

SECTION 10

If you're applying this style with an AI coding assistant, paste the block below as a system prompt or rule set. It encodes the rules in a form models follow well.

# POAST visual style — apply to any web UI you build.

THEMES. Default dark, optional light. Both share the same token names — only
surface and shadow values swap.

DARK SURFACES: bg #06060C, card #09090D, surface #0D0D12, hover #11111A,
border rgba(255,255,255,0.06).
LIGHT SURFACES: bg #FAFAF6, card #FFFFFF, surface #F2F1EC, hover #F6F5F0,
border rgba(0,0,0,0.08).
TEXT (dark): #E8E4DD primary (warm — never #FFFFFF), #8A8690 muted, #4E4B56 dim.
TEXT (light): #1C1A18 primary (warm — never #000000), #6A6770 muted, #A29DA8 dim.

ACCENTS (constant in both themes): amber #F7B041 primary, blue #0B86D1,
teal #2EAD8E success, coral #E06347 danger, violet #905CCB, cyan #26C9D8.
TINTS: accent + low alpha (`#F7B04112` for backgrounds, accent + 25-40%
alpha for borders).

TYPE. 'Outfit' for body (500 weight default, 800 for emphasis).
'Grift' for display headings.
'JetBrains Mono' for tiny caps labels, char counts, IDs.
Body 13-14px. Headings 700+. Caps labels 9-11px mono, uppercase, 1.5-2px
letter-spacing.

RADIUS. 4 chips, 6 pills, 8 buttons + inputs, 10-12 cards, 14 modals,
50% avatars + dots.

SHADOWS (dark). Cards `0 2px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.4)`. Hover adds a 0 0 20px
amber bloom at 8% alpha — never higher. Modals get a subtle 0 0 0 1px
amber ring at 5% alpha.
SHADOWS (light). Cards `0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`. Hover bloom 18% alpha
(slightly higher so it registers against white). Modal ring 15% alpha.

GRADIENTS (only three allowed):
- Card: linear-gradient(135deg, var(--card), var(--surface))
- Surface (reverse): linear-gradient(135deg, var(--surface), var(--card))
- High-energy CTA: linear-gradient(135deg, #F7B041, #26C9D8) with #060608
  800-weight text on top.

STATES.
- Hover: border tints amber ~40% alpha, shadow lifts 1-2px with low-alpha
  amber bloom. 200ms ease.
- Focus: 3px amber halo at 20% alpha + 12-16px outer glow. Border solid
  amber. Visible without color.
- Selected: 5-8% amber wash background, solid amber border at 50% alpha,
  outer glow but no lift.
- Disabled: 40% opacity, no shadow, no transform, cursor not-allowed.

VOICE. No em dashes (commas/periods/colons). No emojis. No hype words:
revolutionary, unleash, game-changing, next-gen, transform, dive into,
unlock, seamless. No "Why X matters" or "Everything you need to know"
openers. Direct, specific, technical sentences. Sentence case for body,
Title Case for proper nouns.

ICONS. lucide-react, stroke 1.8 at rest, 2.2 active. Never filled.

WHEN IN DOUBT. Dark surfaces, single warm accent, low-alpha colored
shadows, off-white type. Restraint over decoration.
