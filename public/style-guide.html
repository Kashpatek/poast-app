Voice & Copy

SECTION 11

Visual style is half the brand. Copy discipline is the other half. Side-by-side: what AI tends to write vs. how POAST should sound.

DON'T · BOOMER / AI
"In this episode, we dive deep into the revolutionary world of AI infrastructure — exploring everything you need to know about how next-gen chips are transforming the future of compute."
"dive deep", "revolutionary", "everything you need to know", "next-gen", "transforming", em dash. Six tells in one sentence.
DO · DIRECT, SPECIFIC
"Doug and Jordan walk through Blackwell yields, why HBM3E is the bottleneck, and what TSMC's CoWoS roadmap means for inference cost in 2026."
Names, numbers, specific stakes. No connective tissue.
DON'T · CLICKBAIT
"Why memory is the next bottleneck (and what nobody's telling you)"
Vague. Withholds the answer. Rhetorical "why" opener.
DO · HARD CLAIM
"HBM3E is sold out through 2026. Here's what that does to GPU pricing."
Concrete claim up top. The reader knows what they're getting.
DON'T · TITLE SCAFFOLD
"A Deep Dive Into Vera Rubin: Everything You Need To Know"
"Deep dive" + "everything you need to know". Two most-flagged AI-coded title scaffolds.
DO · CLAIM AS TITLE
"Vera Rubin Doubles HBM Bandwidth and Halves Power Per Token"
A claim that could only have come from someone who knows the chip.
The hard rules
No em dashes
Use commas, periods, or colons.
No emojis
In product copy. Use lucide icons for symbolic emphasis.
No hype words
Banned: revolutionary, unleash, game-changing, next-gen, transform, dive into, unlock, seamless.
Lead with a fact
Plain declarative sentences. No "Why X matters" rhetorical openers.
Sentence case
Body uses sentence case. Title Case only for proper nouns and tile labels.
Caps labels are mono
JetBrains Mono, 700 weight, 1.5–2px letter-spacing, uppercase. Replaces h3/h4.

AI Pairing

SECTION 13

Paste this as a system prompt for any AI assistant working on a sibling project.

# POAST visual style — apply to any web UI you build.

THEMES. Default dark, optional light. Same token names; surfaces and shadows swap.
DARK: bg #06060C, card #09090D, surface #0D0D12, border rgba(255,255,255,0.06).
LIGHT: bg #FAFAF6, card #FFFFFF, surface #F2F1EC, border rgba(0,0,0,0.08).
TEXT: warm — never #FFFFFF or #000000. Dark: #E8E4DD/#8A8690/#4E4B56. Light: #1C1A18/#6A6770/#A29DA8.
ACCENTS (constant): amber #F7B041 primary, blue #0B86D1, teal #2EAD8E success, coral #E06347 danger,
violet #905CCB, cyan #26C9D8.
TINTS: accent + low alpha (#F7B04112 for backgrounds, accent + 25-40% alpha for borders).

TYPE. Outfit body (500 default, 800 emphasis). Grift display headings.
JetBrains Mono for tiny caps labels, char counts, IDs.
Body 13-14px. Headings 700+. Caps 9-11px mono uppercase, 1.5-2px letter-spacing.

RADIUS. 4 chips, 6 pills, 8 buttons + inputs, 10-12 cards, 14 modals, 50% avatars + dots.

SHADOWS. Dark cards `0 2px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.4)` + 8% amber bloom on hover.
Light cards `0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)` + 18% amber bloom on hover.
Modal ring 5% (dark) or 15% (light) amber.

GRADIENTS (only 3): card, surface, CTA `linear-gradient(135deg, #F7B041, #26C9D8)` with #060608 800-weight text.

STATES. Hover: amber border ~40%, lift 1-2px, low-alpha bloom (200ms ease).
Focus: 3px amber halo at 20% + 12-16px outer glow. Selected: 5-8% amber wash, solid amber border, glow no lift.
Disabled: 40% opacity, no shadow, no transform.

VOICE. No em dashes, no emojis, no hype (revolutionary/unleash/next-gen/transform/dive into/unlock/seamless).
No "Why X matters" / "Everything you need to know" / "Deep dive" openers. Direct, specific, technical.
Sentence case body, Title Case proper nouns.

ICONS. lucide-react, stroke 1.8 rest, 2.2 active. Never filled.

WHEN IN DOUBT. Dark surfaces, single warm accent, low-alpha colored shadows, off-white type. Restraint over decoration.
